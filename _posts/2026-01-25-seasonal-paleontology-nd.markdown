The [Badlands Dinosaur Museum](https://www.dickinsongov.com/museum-center), located on the campus of the Dickinson Museum Center in Dickinson, North Dakota, is hiring for a seasonal paleontology assistant for the summer. 

This is a great summer opportunity for college students studying geology or biology to gain hands-on experience with fossil preparation and museum operations. 

The job description requires applicants to be enrolled "in a post-secondary educational program in a subject appropriate to Paleontology (e.g. Biology, Geology)" or have "equivalent experience in a museum or education setting."

The [full job description](https://www.governmentjobs.com/careers/dickinson?pagetype=jobOpportunitiesJobs&fbclid=IwY2xjawPjiLBleHRuA2FlbQIxMABicmlkETFTbTBRQlVSM2tVdDRWSmwyc3J0YwZhcHBfaWQQMjIyMDM5MTc4ODIwMDg5MgABHkBP9aYnT3TLnfH0FCrYNvNW415N_kKe3pYENq7KhWmtDJe5uMiD89Wv2_HF_aem_H2g7M5cSq5Eot5vZn8mC1w&jobId=5208566&jobName=seasonal-paleontology-assistant&tab=1) is available online. The application deadline is Feb. 12 at 11:59 p.m. Mountain Standard Time.
